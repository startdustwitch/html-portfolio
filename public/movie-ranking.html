The Best Films According to Dee

Films I thoroughly enjoyed

The Mitchells vs. The Machines

The Mitchells are a dysfunctional family.But when their road trip is interrupted by a robot apocalypse,they are forced to get their act together and try to save the world. Will they be able to?

This is one of the best animated adventure comedy films in recent years.

Jathi Rathnalu

This film is an Indian Telugu comedy drama.(I love comedy, haha! We could all use a little humour!)
Three simpletons head to the city looking for jobs to gain dignity and respect in society and...well...things do not go as planned.
This films drips with family-friendly humour,the characters are unique and bizzare, contributing to the craziness of the film. This film is a must watch indeed!

The Breadwinner

An animated ( yup, I love animation too!), drama film about Parvana, an eleven year old girl, and her struggle to provide for her family under the Taliban.
One of the most beautiful films I have ever watched. This film will leave you feeling a mix of indescribable emotions.This is a must watch.
